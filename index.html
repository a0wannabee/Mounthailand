Our Legacy

From the Mountains of Temanggung

Deep in the lush highlands of Temanggung, Central Java, our Robusta coffee trees thrive on mineral-rich volcanic soil and under optimal mountain mist. This exceptional terroir gives Mounthailand Coffee its signature bold body and complex, premium flavor profile.

Every single coffee cherry is harvested by hand, ensuring that only Selected Grade 1 Robusta beans enter our process. We roast our coffee to a precise Medium+ profile, unlocking a deep chocolatey sweetness, low acidity, and an incredibly rich aroma that honors authentic Indonesian heritage.
